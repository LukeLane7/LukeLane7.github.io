HIDDEN WORDS

Welcome to the Frog Rock Country Club!

Heres a little about our staff, how our golf course started, and history that has came through here!

Frog Rock Country Club, invites you to step into a golf world perfect for you and your family. Our rich history, dating back to the 1970s, is a testament to our enduring commitment to providing a top-tier country club experience. We own approximately 90 pristine acres, the Club's journey began with strategic land acquisitions, culminating in the mid-1980s. This country club has witnessed three former presidents, each going to the golf course with a round booked, and one even took the momentous step of getting engaged here. At Frog Rock Country Club, we prioritize the needs of our esteemed members while extending a warm welcome to the public. As a member, you can partake in our exclusive events and gatherings, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie. For those looking to host events, our facilities are available for rent, offering a unique venue for any special occasion. The Club boasts a well-stocked bar, a delightful snack shack, and a common area at the turn, ensuring that you can indulge in both relaxation and refreshment as you navigate the course. One of the distinctive features of Frog Rock is our commitment to enhancing your golfing experience. The golf carts are a complimentary amenity, allowing you to traverse our exquisitely designed course with ease. Our dedication to providing the best possible experience is reflected in our decision to waive any additional fees for these carts. Whether you're a golf enthusiast or simply seeking an oasis of tranquility, Frog Rock Country Club promises an unforgettable and inviting experience for all who choose to grace our grounds. Come and discover the enchanting blend of nature, history, and recreation at Frog Rock Country Club, where cherished memories await you.
