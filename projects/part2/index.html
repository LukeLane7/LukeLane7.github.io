Monthly Masters Mondays (11/6)

Join our Monthly Masters event with a friend!

Starting 10/2 we are starting a masters scramble event at our club every first monday of each month! You and a partner will compete against 49 other teams for a grand prize. You and your team will be competeing on all 18 holes we have to offer. The rules will be laid out the day of, but we will be playing a scramble event. Scramble is best ball out of you and your partner after each shot and using the better one always. Only available to members.
