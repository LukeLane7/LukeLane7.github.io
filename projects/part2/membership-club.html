What is include in our Membership Club?

Here at frog rock we have a beautiful 18-hole course, with a historic Country Club. The Club started purchasing land in the 70's and stopped at around the the mid 80's. Historically we have had 3 presidents play a round at this course. Including one getting engaged right here! We include monthly events and some membership gatherings throughout the year.
